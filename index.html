Eunice Im

Pre-medical Student

I am currently a pre-medical student attending the University of California, Riverside as a Biology major. My career goal is to be a physician, specifically in the specialty of Emergency Medicine. I graduated from Mark Keppel High School in Alhambra, CA and entered UCR with the hopes of becoming a doctor. Currently, I am in the process of finishing required science courses both for graduation as well as for medical school prerequisites. I am involved in many activities on and off campus. I work currently as a medical scribe at Loma Linda Emergency Department, working with physicians in order to accurately chart patients' visits, including but not limited to past history, reason for visit, physical exams, entire ED visit, and further disposition.

I am also involved on campus as the President of Phi Delta Epsilon International Medical Fraternity, an organization that strives to prepare undergraduate students for the future by providing academic resources, workshops and informationals involving medical students, resident physicians, and providers involved with various medical schools. I am also the Vice President of The Literacy Initiative, an on campus organization that spends time each week to read with elementary/middle school students to help increase literacy rates in Riverside. I also work as the Project Leader of Mini Medical School, an organization assocaited with the UCR School of Medicine to help raise knowledge about various health topics in the community, including but not limited to diabetes, hypertension, prenatal health, etc. I also am involved in BPSHI, a club on campus that dedicates time to volunteer with free clinics in the Inland Empire, as well as Project Sunshine, an organization that allows me to spend time volunteering with pediatric patients at Riverside University Health System. Additionally, I have experience working as a Resident Advisor in the UCR Glen Mor Apartments, which has allowed me to developed important interpersonal, management, organizational, and leadership skills to use in the future. I hope to gain further experience in the years to come as well.

In my downtime, I love to watch tv shows and movies, clean, cook, and play piano
